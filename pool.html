FIBERGLASS MONOBLOCK POOLS

Fiberglass swimming pools are one of the most rewarding and customizable ways to transform your backyard into the ultimate outdoor sanctuary. Compared to concrete pools, fiberglass pools are the easiest to maintain and have the strongest and most durable material. When you invest in a fiberglass pool, you’re building a pool that will last a lifetime and remain a constant source of relaxation, fun, beauty, and delight year after year.

Monoblock pools are produced as one piece. It is economical. It is specially designed for detached houses. The pool, which is manufactured as a single piece in our factory, is transported to your place after filtration and accessories are attached.


Advantages of Fiberglass Pool

• Low Maintenance Cost
• Quick Assembly (1 Day Assembly)
• Long Lifespan (10 Years Warranty-30 Years of Use)
• Hygienic Surface
• Compatible with Salt Water Systems.
• Possibility of Transport and Resale/Assembly.

WHY CHOOSE FIBERGLASS MONOBLOCK POOL?

As in concrete pools, there is no algae between the joints, it is hygienic and surface cleaning is much easier. It requires less maintenance and cleaning compared to liner and reinforced concrete pools during use; maintenance costs are less. It has a much longer life than reinforced concrete and prefabricated pools. It is more durable in natural disasters such as ground shaking and landslide. In case the body of the pool is damaged, cracked or broken as a result of any natural disaster or physical accident, it is very practical and 100% guaranteed, repairs are carried out at a very economical cost. It can be easily relocated when desired, and it is a recyclable pool. Compared to a reinforced concrete pool, it is more economical in heating the pool and less costly. Compared to the reinforced concrete pool, the annual pool maintenance chemicals cost is 40% more economical. In the future, when the customer requests a change in the interior color of the pool, a color change can be made in as little as 1 day.

Instead of waiting for the holiday every day of summer, share the happiness of enjoying the pool as if you were on vacation at home! Own a pool in your own home. Own a pool in the garden of your home without the need for concrete ⛱

FIBERGLASS MODULAR POOLS

Modular Pools can be made in any desired size. Parts of the desired pool are produced in our factory, packed and transported safely. The joints are leveled so that the joints are not visible. Especially pools of different forms and large sizes are made in this way. It is manufactured as a Skimer Type Modular Pool.

Thanks to these pool systems, you can quickly have the most ideal size pool. Moreover, a more beautiful and practical pool can be built with more affordable prices compared to other pool options. The demounted pools are designed according to the size of the desired area and moved to the relevant area. Thanks to the system installed in the opened suitable area, the pool installation process is carried out quickly without waiting for the concrete pouring process.

Fiberglass pool is a pool made of gelcoat, resin and fiberglass mat, also known as glass reinforced polyester (GRP). Their surfaces are quite smooth. The inside of the fiberglass material is covered with polyester resin and consists of many threads.It is also known as glass fiber reinforced polyester or fiber pools due to the material from which they are produced. Prefabricated Pools are designed at the factory and the thing to do is to install this pool in a living space. Owning a private prefabricated pool is preferred in small and complex areas, thanks to the fact that they are pre-manufactured.

WHY CHOOSE FIBERGLASS MODULAR POOL?

It is one of the easiest, fastest and most economical ways to own a pool. Fiberglass modular pool is produced in our factory and comes ready to be applied in the form of several pieces. Its application is extremely easy and effortless. You can include a prefabricated pool among your options to add comfort and pleasure to your living spaces. The fact that your living spaces such as a garden or terrace are relatively small does not mean that you cannot have a pool. GRP pools, which reduce the construction cost and manufacturing process by half, can be used easily on roofs, terraces, gardens, indoors and outdoors, since there is no insulation problem. Prefabricated Pools have also become a trend around the world. Ergonomic, robustly designed ready-made pools with quick and easy installation have transformed the personal pool from pure luxury into an accessible product. Modular pools with freedom of size and shape are heavily preferred due to the short manufacturing process.
