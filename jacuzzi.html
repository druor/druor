WHY CHOOSE POLYESTER MODULAR POOL?
Among the composite plastic materials, the most known and most used is glass fiber reinforced polyester, known as fiber-glass or GRP. FRP is a material used in many sectors such as aircraft, space shuttle, automotive, food and energy today. The reason why it is such a popular material is that it is light, durable and the manufacturing process is fast. CTP pools, known as package pools or modular pools, have started to be in demand today for these reasons. GRP pools, which reduce the construction cost and manufacturing process by half, can be used easily in roofs, terraces, gardens, indoor and outdoor spaces due to the absence of any insulation problem. Modular pools with freedom of size and shape are preferred intensely due to the short manufacturing process.
